I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Robotics at Northwestern University. I graduated from Purdue University in May 2019, where I earned my B.S. in Mechanical engineering and The Certificate of Entrepreneurship and Innovation. After graduating, I gained hands on experience in robotic research and design at The Institute for Human & Machine Cognition (IHMC) in Pensacola, Florida, where I prototyped and assembled the legs and feet of the IHMC's very own hydraulic powered humanoid robot. My passion for robotics and desire to expand my skillset to more than just mechanics brought me to the Northwestern University MSR program.
